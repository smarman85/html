Welcome to My Site

I have been working in the field for a little over a year now. At the year mark I was not where I wanted to be so I decided to enroll in Dev BootCamp to increase my skills. Over the next few months I will be posting a lot of the stuff I learn to this site. I hope you enjoy it.

I am a Jr. Interactive Developer working in Sunny Southern California. After college I had a few random jobs before I landed on Web Development. Using my very limited experience with HTML and CSS, I decided to take a few classes at my local city college to pick up some more info.

I tried to focus a little on front-end and back-end dev, just to see what I liked better. After spending some time in both arenas, I really got hooked on back-end development. Its fascinating to be see to all the behind the scenes work like logging user info in a database and making sites truly dynamic.
